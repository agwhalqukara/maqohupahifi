Jack Frost Nipping At Your Nose Lyrics

Nate King Cole‘s timeless rendition of The Christmas Song instantly transports listeners into the enchanting spirit of the holiday season.

With its warm, velvety tones and heartfelt delivery, Cole’s voice becomes the melodic embodiment of winter nights by the crackling fireplace.

Facts About Jack Frost Nipping At Your Nose Lyrics.

FactDescription
Song TitleThe Christmas Song
ArtistNat King Cole
Release Year1946
GenreJazz, Christmas
SongwriterMel Tormé and Robert Wells
Album“The Magic of Christmas” (later reissued as “The Christmas Song”)
Chart PerformanceThe song was not initially a major hit but gained popularity over the years. It eventually became a Christmas standard and a signature song for Nat King Cole.
RecordingThe original recording of the song was made by Nat King Cole on June 14, 1946, with the Nat King Cole Trio.
Notable CoversNumerous artists have covered “The Christmas Song,” including Frank Sinatra, Ella Fitzgerald, Christina Aguilera, and Michael Bublé.
Grammy Hall of Fame InductionIn 1974, “The Christmas Song” was inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame.
