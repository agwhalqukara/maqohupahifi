Meet the Cast of Dubai Bling on Netflix: Find Them on Instagram!

The new show on Netflix, Dubai Bling, has gathered a lot of viewers’ attention and people have been seeking information about the cast members. The show premiered on October 27, 2022, and follows the personal and professional lives of some of Dubai, UAE’s wealthiest people. Follow to know more about them with their Instagram handle.

Everyone enjoys a little bit of glitz and glamour in their life, and thanks to Netflix, people can do so while relaxing in their own homes. Fans will get the opportunity to observe how the wealthy of Dubai live as the platform premieres the new show following the success of Bling Empire.

Dubai Bling follows the personal and professional lives of some of Dubai, UAE’s wealthiest people. The majority of the cast members frequently interact with one another because they belong to the same social circle. As might be expected, some characters have troubled histories with one another and engage in dramatic confrontations during the course of the series.

Cast Members of Dubai Bling: Meet All the Contestants of the New Netflix Show and Find Them on Instagram!

Dubai Bling has its share of heartwarming moments, thanks to the bonds of friendship and love, but it also has its fair measure of entertainment. As mentioned right above, the show features eight well-known Dubai residents/casts and their opulent lifestyles. You’ve come to the right place if you wonder about the cast members of the show.

Loujain Adadah

Loujain Adadah (@loujainaj), who was born in California but moved to her native Lebanon as a youngster, studied there as an adult. Her passions have always been travel, photography, and fashion. When she was just 14 years old, she was given her first magazine ad shoot. After that, she continued to model and was inundated with offers for commercials. She was previously married to Wailn Juffali.

Adadah received multiple TV opportunities as a result of her stage appearance, and she later rose to the position of host of the Energy Spin Magazine music show on MTV Lebanon. Today, she is a loving mother of two and has amassed over 110k Instagram followers.

Farhana Bodi

Farhana Bodi (@farhanabodi) was born in India and has been living in Dubai since 2008. She was raised in South Africa. She can speak English, Hindi, Gujarati, and Urdu because of her upbringing. She was initially a tomboy who disliked the fashion industry. However, when she turned 19, things changed because she started working as a makeup artist and model.

When Farhana started I Woman of the World, her lifestyle blog, she found success. She now has 1.4 million Instagram followers as a result of being able to grow in the online world.

Ebraheem Al Samadi

Since an early age, Ebraheem Al Samadi (@thebloomingman) has been an entrepreneur. When he was 14 years old, he launched an eBay store as his first foray into commerce. His perseverance eventually paid off as he made numerous sales and garnered 1000 Power Sellers honors.

At the age of 17, he also started his own roller-skating shoe business. He became a billionaire at the age of 20 thanks to his accomplishments. In 2010, he relocated to Dubai and assumed leadership of the Al Samadi Group.

Zeina Khoury

Zeina Khoury (@thezeinakhoury), who was born in Lebanon, moved to Dubai in 2006 after earning her degree from Notre Dame University. Despite having a degree in banking and finance, she chose to pursue a different path.

She started working in real estate and built the five-star Palazzo Versace Dubai. She purchased High Mark Real Estate in 2009 as a result of her hard work and perseverance. She currently serves as the company’s CEO, chief growth officer, and TV and radio personality.

Safa Siddiqui

Safa Siddiqui (@safa_dubai) is an Iraqi who was born and raised in London. She has successfully carved out a name for herself over her eight years in Dubai. She is married to Fahad Siddiqui. She used to work in real estate but is now retired and concentrates on being a fantastic mother. She also has over 32,000 followers on social media.

Kris Fade

Kris Fade (@krisfade) is the creator of Fade Fit, a protein snack. Additionally, he founded and serves as host of the Virgin Radio Dubai radio program Kris Fade Show. He also maintains a significant social media presence, with over 300,000 Instagram followers. He frequently creates interesting and amusing stuff for his fans on the platform.

DJ Bliss

According to his website, DJ Bliss (@djblissdubai) began DJing in the 1990s and fell in love with the job. He applied for his first job at a theme park and self-taught himself how to operate the equipment. He later took over as the show’s host on Dubai One Channel. Since then, he has given numerous performances and amassed a sizable fan base.

Lojain Omran

A lifestyle influencer with more than 10 million followers on social media, Lojain Omran (@lojain_omran) is a television personality. Her social media posts primarily focus on fashion as she flaunts her opulent lifestyle and stunning designer attire. She also actively promotes humanitarian causes and frequently shares content to inspire people.
