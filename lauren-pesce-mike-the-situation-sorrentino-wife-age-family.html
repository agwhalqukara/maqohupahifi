Lauren Pesce (Mike The Situation Sorrentino Wife) Age, Family

Lauren Pesce is a reality star, writer, and realtor. She runs ‘The StyleBAE,’ a blog she uses to impart opinions on fashion, fitness, food, and life in general. Moreover, Pesce was also a star on Marriage Boot Camp, a show that airs on WE TV.

Lauren’s fame is boosted by the fact that she is the wife of Mike Sorrentino (The Situation), who is famous for his own reality show titled “Jersey Shore.” Let us take a deeper look in the day-to-day livelihood of Lauren Pesce, examining her age and family in the process.

Lauren married Mike Sorrentino

Lauren is the wife of the popular reality star, Mike Sorrentino. The couple met at Brookdale Community College while they were both students. They dated for nearly two years before Mike landed a role on the Jersey Shore.

Once Mike got this opportunity, he thought about taking a break from their relationship so that he could focus on his career. However, he opted against this idea, and their relationship continued.

The couple dated for close to 15 years before Mike decided to propose to Lauren in 2018. Lauren used her Instagram handle to relay the good news to the rest of the world. Mike even went further and surprised his wife with a lovely slide show from the Jersey Shore. By April 26th, 2018, Lauren and Mike were exchanging vows at the Legacy Castle in Pompton Plains.

Soon after his career kicked off, Mike developed a drug problem, which eventually led to prison time. Throughout it all, Lauren remained by his side, even when things got really hard. More misfortune followed the couple in 2019, as Lauren suffered a miscarriage when she was 7 weeks pregnant.

Fast forward to December 2020, and the couple announced that Lauren was pregnant with a baby boy. He is expected to arrive in May 2021. They relayed this positive news through Instagram.
