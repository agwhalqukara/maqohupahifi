The Mire Millennium Season 3 Plot: What can it be About?

In the late 1980s, “The Mire” follows the investigation surrounding the murders of a local communist activist and a prostitute in the Gronty forest. Veteran journalist Witold Wanycz, alongside his new assistant Piotr Zarzycki, embarks on an unofficial investigation while covering the story for the regional newspaper. Season 2, “The Mire ’97,” shifts to 1997, when a flood reveals human remains and the body of a recently deceased teenage boy in the same forest. Sergeant Anna Jass takes on the investigation into the boy’s death. In Season 3, “The Mire Millennium,” set on the brink of the new millennium, old cases gain new context, and the past catches up with the main characters. As they face new crimes and unusual clues, they ultimately uncover intriguing plotlines.

The Mire Millennium Season 3 Cast: Who’s on board?

Dawid Ogrodnik takes on the role of Piotr Zarzycki, while Andrzej Seweryn portrays Witold Wanycz. Zofia Wichłacz embodies the character of Teresa Zarzycka, with Magdalena Walach as Helena Grochowiak and Agnieszka Zulewska as Nadia. Zbigniew Walerys and Dennis Kleinman play Zbigniew Brynski, while Patrick Boylan portrays another iteration of Piotr Zarzycki. New additions to the cast comprise Filip Pławiak from “The Defence,” Tomasz Schuchardt known for “High Water,” Konrad Eleryk from “Infamy,” Tomasz Sapryk also from “High Water,” Wojciech Kalarus known for “1983,” Maria Kania from “The Convict,” Michalina Łabacz from “The Teacher,” and Antoni Sałaj from “The Woods.”
