Heidi Montag Attempts to Recreate Kim Kardashian's Bikini Booty Shoot

Cheeky! Heidi Montag took a page out of Kim Kardashian’s playbook on Thursday, Aug. 20, posing in a racy, Kardashian-inspired photo shoot in honor of her husband Spencer Pratt’s 32nd birthday. 

The Hills alum, 28, showed off her curves in a series of brightly colored bikinis while vacationing in Hawaii. Channeling her inner Kardashian in one blue suit, Montag looked over her shoulder as her booty hung over a wooden boat oar.

The pose is almost identical to one done by Kanye West’s wife on the family’s televised trip to Thailand in April 2014. Kardashian, 34, who was proud of her newly slimmed down body after welcoming daughter North West the previous June, had posed in a flesh-toned two-piece for a racy beach photo shoot. 

Montag didn’t have quite as much junk in her trunk, but she and Pratt made up for that by posing for several other racy pictures, including pictures of them smooching on the beach.

The attention-loving couple also has a link to the Kardashians in Hills pal Brody Jenner. The son of Caitlyn Jenner and Linda Thompson has appeared on several seasons of Keeping Up With the Kardashians.
