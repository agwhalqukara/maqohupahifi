Vanessa Hudgens: Ashley Tisdale Looked Like an &quot;Angel&quot; at Her Wedding

Wildcats forever! More than eight years ago, Vanessa Hudgens and Ashley Tisdale first starred in High School Musical together. This month, Hudgens attended Tisdale's wedding. 

"It was so beautiful!" the Gimme Shelter actress, 25, tells Us Weekly about Tisdale's secret September nuptials. "I bawled my eyes out."

The blonde beauty, 29, married her musician love Christopher French on Monday, Sept. 8, after getting engaged last August. 

"She looked like an angel," adds Hudgens, who was one of Tisdale's six bridesmaids. The bride wore a stunning white wedding dress with cap sleeves and a voluminous skirt. Her blonde hair was down in waves and adorned with a flower crown.
