'I Wanted Revenge'Jerry Hall Admits to Cheating on Ex Mick Jagger After He Had an Affair

Jerry Hall is coming clean about her tumultuous, 23-year relationship with Mick Jagger.

In a revealing new interview, the 59-year-old former model confessed she once had an affair a man named Robert Sangster to seek revenge on her partner Mick, 72, after she found out he was cheating on her with Italian model Carla Bruni.

“I did [want revenge]. At one point I went off and had an affair with somebody else. Robert Sangster. So lovely. Horse breeder. Then Mick would beg me back,” she told the Radio Times.

Jerry and Mick with one of their four children in January 1990.

“I thought Mick would eventually change, but he didn’t… I mean he’s Mick Jagger and millions of girls throw themselves at him, and he’s in the position where he says yes,” she added.

Following her relationship with Sangster, Jerry and Mick — who began dating in late 1977 — got back together and soon after the actress was expecting the couple’s first child, daughter Elizabeth Scarlett Jagger, 31. The pair later married in a Hindu beach ceremony in Indonesia in November 1990 and had three more children, daughter Georgia May Jagger, 23, and sons James Leroy Jagger, 30, and Gabriel Luke Jagger, 18. Jerry and Mick ended their common-law marriage in 1999 with an annulment.
