KaDee Strickland Pregnant, Expecting First Child With Jason Behr

KaDee Strickland and Jason Behr have a baby on the way! The actress — whose character, Charlotte King, welcomed triplets during the final season of Private Practice — is pregnant with her first child, Strickland's rep tells Us Weekly.

The Fever Pitch actress, 37, and her Breakout Kings husband, 39, will become parents this fall. The actors met on the set of the 2004 movie The Grudge, and Behr popped the question the following year.
