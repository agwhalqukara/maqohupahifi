Sophie Skelton Puts Her Curves On Full Display In Breathtakingly Beautiful Sheer Gown At The Outland

Sophie Skelton is being called a fairy-tale princess, a goddess, and the most stunning to appear on any red carpet this season after making her debut at the Season 5 Outlander premiere. Wearing Georges Hobeika Spring/Summer 2020 Couture, the 25-year-old beauty was stunning in the sheer gown. The dress’ bodice is made of intricately braided tulle that gives the dress a nearly woven effect. Free-flowing panels of tulle flow from the shoulders and dress’ skirt that features high slits on both sides. From the front view, you can’t completely see that this dress is revealing, but it is. With the sheer paneling and the high slits, Sophie’s long legs and curves were on full display.

You may see a photo of the dress as shared on the official Georges Hobeika Instagram site where two models are wearing both tulle dresses. If you look closely to the bodice, you can see that the dress is actually composed of braided tulle making this a very unique and original look.

The pastel colors of lilac, aqua, and lemon-yellow give this dress a dream-like feel and it’s no wonder people are comparing Sophie to a fairytale princess and goddess.
