Obituary: Darrell Jones

Passing of Darrell Jones

It is with deep sadness that we announce the passing of Darrell Jones, a beloved resident of Lampasas, Texas. On December 27, 2023, at the age of 86, Darrell Jones left this world, leaving behind a void that will be felt by all who knew him. His departure has left a profound impact on the community, and his memory will forever be cherished.

Family and Friends Left Behind


Darrell Jones was a cherished husband, father, grandfather, and great-grandfather. He was surrounded by a loving family who will deeply miss his presence. His children, Pete Jones, Bob Jones, DeAnn Holden, Kimberly, Anna Coker, and Amy LaPoe, were his pride and joy. Their lives were enriched by his love, guidance, and unwavering support.

In addition to his immediate family, Darrell Jones is survived by his former life partner, Gilma Flatt, and his brother Troy Jones. He was preceded in death by his wife Shelba Dean Miller, his brothers Doug, Hiram, and Allen, his sisters-in-law Ann, Linda, and Thurma Nell, his daughter-in-law Rosemary Jones, and his great-grandchild Mason Van Zandt. His parents, Luther D. Jones (Luke) and Lena Elizabeth Jones (Green), were also among those who passed away before him.

Darrell Jones’s legacy extends beyond his immediate family. He leaves behind 17 grandchildren, 16 great-grandchildren, and four great-great-grandchildren, all of whom will carry his memory in their hearts. His impact on their lives will continue to shape their futures, and his values and teachings will be passed down through generations.
