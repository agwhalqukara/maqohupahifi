Paulinas Holiday Hospital Stay, How Bad Will Her Medical Emergency Be?

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers for the week of December 18th suggest Paulina Price (Jackée Harry) won’t have a holly jolly Christmas. She’s always been a busy lady, so being tired comes with the territory, but when her daughter Chanel Dupree (Raven Bowens) notices this trend of fatigue, she’ll take her mom straight to the hospital.

DOOL Spoilers: Paulina Price Will Resist

DOOL spoilers hint that Chanel has seen her mom complain about being tired a lot as of late. During the week of December 18th, Chanel will ask Paulina to go Christmas shopping, but her mom will decline due to work.

Ms. Dupree will later find Paulina asleep on the couch again and realize she’s downplaying her fatigue. She’ll believe a doctor’s visit is in order; however, Ms. Price will be resistant to the idea.

Still, Chanel will be far more stubborn on the issue, and she’ll end up taking her mom to the hospital for a check-up. After all, Salem’s mayor needs to be in tip-top shape, right?

Days Spoilers: Will Paulina Price Be Okay?

Days spoilers indicate that Paulina will be examined by a doctor, but will they be able to diagnose her condition right away?

Blood and other tests will likely be needed, so it may take days (pun intended) before Chanel and her mom find out if there’s any concern needed.

Fatigue is linked to a lot of illnesses, so it’s hard to say if what Paulina has is serious. Maybe it’s linked to stress?

Not only is she Salem’s mayor, but she’s also still worried about her hubby Abe Carver (James Reynolds) who hasn’t fully recovered from his amnesia.

Still, if Paulina’s condition is serious, there’s a good chance that she’ll hide it from Chanel and Abe.

She tried to hide her tiredness from her daughter, to begin with, and will likely work overtime to cover up any awful illness she has to minimize Chanel’s heartache.
