JoJo Siwa Wins Social Media Personality at the CelebMix Awards

You have been voting for your favourite social media personalities for a month and now we have found a winner. With 70 % of the votes, your favourite social media personality is JoJo Siwa.

JoJo Siwa is a 13-year-old singer, actress, and dancer best known for her appearances on the reality show ‘Dance Moms’. The young star first showed off her bubbly personality during the second season of ‘Abby’s Ultimate Dance Competition’. Although she didn’t win, JoJo went on to become a permanent member of the Abby Lee Dance Company’s elite team. The dancer left ‘Dance Moms’ earlier this year but her career has shown no signs of slowing down. JoJo continues to wow everyone with her bright persona via Twitter and YouTube where she does regular vlogs.

Congratulations JoJo Siwa!
