- A truly hilarious clip of a man getting attacked by ducks has been shared online

- In the clip, the guy, who is wearing incredibly high heels, models for the camera before the ducks start attacking him

Imagine dressing up in your fanciest heels so that you can feel yourself in front of a camera and then getting attacked by some ducks? A young man - who apparently has aspirations of being a drag queen - recently hilariously experienced this first-hand.

In a clip that was shared on Twitter by funny clips plug ChristoThurston, a young man who put on his finest dress and highest heels to pose for the camera suddenly gets attacked by a number of ducks who were initially minding their own business.

"It is a wuuuu shem video," Christo captioned the terribly funny clip online.

In other funny Briefly News stories, is the apocalypse near? That is the major question on the mind of a worried US homeowner. Cristina Ryan, a resident of Palm Coast, is currently living in fear after seeing a creature looking like a dinosaur running across her yard.

The incident made Ryan question whether the prehistoric creatures are creeping back years after alleged extinction. According to Fox 35 News, Ryan disclosed to the media that her security camera recorded what seemed like a baby dinosaur crossing her yard.

"Any animal we can come up with that would be 'walking' at 3:40am in the morning, wouldn't walk this way. Maybe I have watched 'Jurassic Park' too many times, but I see a raptor or other small dinosaur!" Ryan told Fox 35 News.
