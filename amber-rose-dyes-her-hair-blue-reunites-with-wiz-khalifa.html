Amber Rose Dyes Her Hair Blue, Reunites With Wiz Khalifa

Feeling blue?! Amber Rose has jumped on the Hilary Duff train and dyed her hair blue. The former model debuted her new look while stepping out at Penthouse nightclub on Sunday, April 5, in West Hollywood, Calif.

Before hitting up the club in a plunging white ensemble, Rose reunited with her estranged husband Wiz Khalifa to celebrate Easter with their 2-year-old son Sebastian. 

According to TMZ, the exes were affectionate with each other at Tamar Braxton’s Easter party in Calabasas. The couple, who recently settled their nasty custody battle, were reportedly spotted hugging and being playful.

Just last week, Rose declared her love for her ex in an emotional Instagram.
